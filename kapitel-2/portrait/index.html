Kurt Cobain

Kurt Cobain was the lead singer, guitarist, and primary songwriter for the rock band Nirvana. Born on February 20, 1967, he became an iconic figure of the 1990s rock music scene, representing the grunge movement. He is known for his raw, emotional lyrics and unique vocal style. Cobain tragically passed away in 1994, leaving behind a lasting legacy in the music world.
