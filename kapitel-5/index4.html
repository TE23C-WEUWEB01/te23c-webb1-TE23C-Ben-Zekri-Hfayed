Arctic Monkeys

Arctic Monkeys is an influential British rock band formed in 2002 in Sheffield, England. Known for their sharp lyrics, dynamic sound, and charismatic performances, they helped define the indie rock revival of the 2000s. Combining elements of post-punk, garage rock, and later more experimental styles, their music often explores themes of youth, relationships, and modern life, making them a defining voice for a generation of indie music fans.
