Taco Hemingway

Taco Hemingway is an influential Polish rapper and songwriter who rose to fame in the 2010s. Known for his clever lyrics and relatable storytelling, he became a key voice in Poland's modern hip-hop scene. Taco's music blends rap with spoken word, tackling themes like city life, relationships, and societal struggles, making him a defining figure for younger Polish generations.
