Lana Del Rey

Lana Del Rey is an influential American singer-songwriter who emerged in the early 2010s. Known for her cinematic style, melancholic lyrics, and vintage-inspired sound, she blends elements of pop, indie rock, and Americana. Her music often explores themes of love, heartbreak, nostalgia, and the darker side of fame, making her a unique and defining voice of modern alternative music.
