Nirvana

Nirvana was an influential American rock band formed in 1987 in Aberdeen, Washington. The band is best known for pioneering the grunge music movement of the late 1980s and early 1990s, which combined elements of punk rock, alternative rock, and heavy metal. Their raw sound, introspective lyrics, and rebellious attitude made them a defining voice of Generation X.
