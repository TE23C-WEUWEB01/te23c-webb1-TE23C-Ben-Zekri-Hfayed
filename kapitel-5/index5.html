Wanda i Banda

Wanda i Banda is a popular Polish rock band formed in 1982 and led by charismatic vocalist Wanda Kwietniewska. Known for their energetic performances and catchy songs, the band blends elements of rock, pop, and new wave. Their music often features powerful vocals and lively melodies, making them a staple of Polish rock in the 1980s and beyond. Some of their most famous hits include *"Hi-Fi"* and *"Nie będę Julią."*
